How much is your
classic car worth?
Our free valuation tool provides accurate regional estimates on classics and future classics. Using global and local sale history, current and past market history on your classics gives you a better idea of what your vehicle is worth now and in the future.
Valuation Data
Valuation is broken down at the root level based on a car’s quality. The exact same model car may sell for far higher values simply based on condition alone. Factors such as originality, milage, service and care of the car can all contribute to this. We rate vehicles as either project grade, fair, good, or showroom. Showroom cars are those where every fine detail has been addressed and are ready to cross the worlds most prominent auction blocks, while projects are maybe not fully intact or running.
